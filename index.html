Stiperstones under the stars

At the end of August, my dad wanted to head out for some landscape photography

The sky was clear which didn't make for a great sunset but we decided to camp out in the van whilst we waited for the stars to show

I am glad we hung on as it's rare in this country for the skies to be perfectly clear and no moon.

What a night! One for the wall!
